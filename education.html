About Me

I grew up in Kingston, Jamaica. I migrated to the United States after high school. I am very curious and enjoy learning. As a result, I pursue degrees in Science, Psychology, and Business Administration. I also dedicated my time to serving in the United States Army as a Medic. After, honorable discharge from the Army, I now work for the City of Phoenix for approximately 15 years.

To continue my quest for knowledge, I have completed a Full Stack Web Development program with University of Arizona. I have finally found my passion for life in the technology field. I can now capitalize on my creative skills, problem solving, attention to detail, etc.

In my spare time, I enjoy spending time with my family, hiking, attending church activities, Netflix binge watching, work on various types of art projects, do major home projects such as toilet installations, wood floor installations, door installations, landscape projects, etc.
